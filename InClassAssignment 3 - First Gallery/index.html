Sunday Night Horror Movies Recomendations

The Shinning (1980)

A novelist takes a job as winter caretaker of the isolated, old, huge and beautiful Overlook Hotel. Jack brings his wife and his son to live along in the . It happens that Danny, has a mysterious power known as "The Shining".

Hellraiser (1987)

Larry decides to move into his mother's old house with his new family. After accidently triggering his dead brother's resurrection they also encounter the Cenobites and their creepy leader.

The Omen (1976)

This very particular film follows "the omen" of a child who was switch at birth with the Ambassador's and his wife's son by a sect, killing their real son. Granting the son of satan to grow up with wealth and power.

The Evil Dead (1981)

Five friends go up to a cabin in the woods, where they find unspeakable evil lurking in the forest. They find a tome called the "Necronomicon", Book of the Dead, and the taped translation of the text. Once the tape is played, the evil is released.

The Ring (2002)

Just like an urban legend, a cursed videotape that would lead to a phone call foretelling the viewer's death in exactly seven days. A newspaper reporter tracks down the video and watches it. Now she has just seven days to unravel the mystery.

Martyrs (2008)

Two young women who were both victims of abuse as children embark on a bloody quest for revenge only to find themselves plunged into a living hell of depravity.
