Madroño.

/maˈdɾoɲo/ noun. A traditional Nicaraguan cuisine venue, serving the best dishes and drinks from across the range of Nicaraguan cuisines. A definitive neighborhood joint in South Miami and Miami Lakes, offering a laid-back dining experience in a casual setting to guests from all walks of life.

A Local Family Tradition

Over two decades ago a group of Nicaragua sibilings living in Miami decided to create a restaurant that would feature the finest Nicaraguan dishes in an ambiance representative of its Spanish colonial architecture. They ambitioned it to be a place where people would be exposed to the Nicaraguan traditions of fine cuisine, personalized service and festive entertainment. In 1985 Madroño Restaurant came to fruition and thus a Nicaraguan home was transformed into the premier Latin steakhouse.

Adhering to true Nicaraguan philosophy we seek only to offer the very best to our customers. The finest cut of beef has always been the tenderloin, which is precisely what is served to satisfy our guest´s appetites. Our now famed churrasco steak is a very lean tenderloin, from grass fed cattle, cut and prepared in a special Nicaraguan manner. Our menu is rich like our heritage, also offering seafood, poultry, and other meats, each prepared in unique local styles. The marinades, sauces and spices are all genuine Central American creations. Since Nicaraguans are famous for their hospitality, the same care taken in our food is transferred to our surroundings, service, and entertainment.

We believe that...

How your food is served and the attention paid to the service is as important as what is being our hig quality food. We focus promptly in hosting an experience rather than just another night eating out.
